LET US DO THE CALCULATIONS!

Presenting CARBCONcalator!

Your one-stop weekly food carbon footprint calculator with multiple food categories to ease your calculations.

Just enter your food portion (kgs), select a food item for each category, and get the individual or collective result.*

* The carbon footprint value represents kg of CO2 emitted for per kg of food item

Disclaimers


  1. The calculator does not take into account your food preferences and hence would like the user to make a decision
  2. It does not consider or calculate how a person may reduce their carbon footprint by changing their food choices
  3. The validity of the results depends on the appropriateness of the assumptions and the accuracy of the data
